# **Blade Runner Archetype Quiz**

A cyberpunk-inspired interactive personality quiz based on the archetypes, themes, and philosophical ideas explored in the Blade Runner universe.  
This project combines:  
* Narrative archetype analysis  
* Interactive web design  
* Cyberpunk-inspired UI/UX
* Personality quiz systems
* Blade Runner thematic exploration
The quiz assigns users to a unique Blade Runner archetype and corresponding faction alignment based on their responses.


## **Features**

* Interactive Personality Quiz
* 20-question personality system
* Multiple-choice branching responses
* Dynamic archetype scoring system
* Blade Runner Archetypes

The quiz includes archetypes inspired by characters and themes from:  
Blade Runner (1982)  
Blade Runner 2049 (2017)  


## **Faction System**

The archetypes are divided into three major thematic factions inspired by the core philosophical conflicts of the Blade Runner universe.  

🧬 The Seekers  
Themes: Identity • Memory • Emotion • Belonging  
These archetypes focus on self-discovery, emotional authenticity, memory, loneliness, and the search for meaning.  
Archetypes:  
* Manufactured Being Seeking Humanity
* Hidden Chosen One
* Lost Child
* Memory Architect
* Artificial Companion
* Archivist of Truth
* Mentor in Exile
* Last Relic of the Past
* Test Subject
* Ethical Scientist

🏙️ The Makers & Masters  
Themes: Power • Creation • Authority • Control  
These archetypes represent creators, rulers, enforcers, and individuals who shape or maintain systems of power.  
Archetypes:  
* Creator-God
* Tyrannical Visionary
* Corporate Enforcer
* Femme Fatale Enforcer
* Broken Lawman

🔥 The Rebels  
Themes: Rebellion • Sacrifice • Transformation • Evolution  
These archetypes challenge oppressive systems and embody revolution, sacrifice, freedom, and transcendence.  
Archetypes:  
* Fallen Angel
* Prophet of a New World
* Underground Resistance
* Disillusioned Hunter
* Mother of a New Species

## **How the Quiz Works**

* The user answers personality-based questions.
* Each answer contributes points toward specific archetypes.
* Scores are calculated dynamically.
* The highest-scoring archetype is selected.
* The archetype is matched to a faction.
* A cyberpunk-themed result card is generated.
* Archetype Inspiration

The project draws inspiration from major Blade Runner characters such as:
* Roy Batty
* K
* Rachael
* Joi
* Niander Wallace
* Deckard
* Freysa
* Luv
These characters were transformed into broader symbolic archetypes representing identity, morality, rebellion, creation, and emotional authenticity.
